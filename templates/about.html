ABOUT

Recently done a coding course, loved it? But feeling a bit lost now it's over? Our meetup group, Keep Calm and Carry on Coding, is for you. Come with a project and work on it in the sessions we organise. If you don't have it, don't worry, make an online CV your starting point and bring that along.
